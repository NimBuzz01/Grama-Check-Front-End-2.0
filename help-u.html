Guide to Grama Check

User Guide

Apply Certificate

1. Click on the “Apply Now” button in the Home Page.

2. Then Sign-in or create an account in the Grama Check Application.

3. After then Enter your details and upload the Images that are required and then submit the information by clicking the “Submit” button.

Check Status

1. Click on the “Apply Now” button in the Home Page.

2. Then Sign-in or create an account in the Grama Check Application.

3. After then click on the “Click Here” button in the Check Status Now field.

4. Enter your NIC number and click on the “Submit” button, after then you can check your certification status.
